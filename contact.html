Lab Location

How to Find Us

Our lab is located in Building A on the main campus. We are on the third floor, Room 301. The building is easily accessible from the main entrance.

Directions:

  1. Enter the main campus from University Drive
  2. Follow the signs to Building A
  3. Take the elevator or stairs to the 3rd floor
  4. Our lab is located at the end of the hallway

Parking:

Visitor parking is available in Lot B, adjacent to Building A. Parking permits can be obtained from the main office.
